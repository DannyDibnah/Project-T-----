Transition

These are methods of transition.

The Social Transition

Name Change

Name change can include simply going by a new name, or getting a legal name change on all documentation.

Pronoun Change

An individual will ask the people in their life to start using the correct pronouns. (Such as with an FTM, people would start using "He" rather than "She"

Clothing

The individual will start dressing in the clothing they feel comfortable wearing.

Coming Out

The person will come out to the people around them and start living as their affirmed gender.

The Medical Transition

Hormones

A person will start HRT (Hormone Replacement Therapy) to cause physical chanfes that align witht their affirmed gender.

These can include Testosterone and Estrogen pills, injections, and gels.

Top Surgery

This surgery allows for the person to create a more masculine or feminine chest

Examples: Mastectomy and breast augmentation

Bottom Surgery

These operations allow for a person to change the appearance of their reproductive organs

Example: Phalloplasty

Other surgeries

Some of these are facial reconsturction surgeries, Adam's apple reduction, electrolysis, and vocal surgery.

Transition in general

Other ways of "passing"

Some people bind their chests or darken their facial hair in order to "pass" as male. Some transwomen shave their legs and faces, and wear wigs to "pass" as female.
